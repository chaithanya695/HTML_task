Chicken Curry

Ingredients

  • Chicken 1 kg
  • Chopped Onions
  • Chopped Green chillies
  • Ginger Paste
  • Turmeric powder
  • Garlic
  • Cumin seeds
  • Bay leaves
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Chilli Powder
  • Chicken Masala
  • Corriender leaves
  • Curry leaves
  • Garam Masala

Instructions

  1. Light on the Stove
  2. Take a bowl and pour some oil
  3. Add cumin seeds
  4. After some time add chopped onions and green chillies to it
  5. When onions becomes brownish then add genger paste and turmeric powder, mix it well
  6. After 10 minutes add raw chicken to it and mix it thoroughly
  7. Cook it under low flame for nearly 30 minutes
  8. Then add required salt, red chilli powder to it
  9. Boil it for 10 minutes and add bayleaves, chicken masala, corriender leaves, curry leaves and garam masala to it
  10. Mix it well and cook it for another 10 minutes
  11. Now! Chicken curry is ready to serve üòÅ
